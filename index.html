Puente Genil

Come with me and I will show a piece of my town!

Puente Genil is a town located in Córdoba and its Holy Week is really famous in all Andalusia

A lot of people come during march and april, looking for the "tronos", which represent every capitle of the Bible

It is really fascinating to see all the crowd looking the figure of Jesus and María and hearing the band that follows the "trono".

History

Holy Week in Puente Genil was invented around 500 years ago, and it's something that has never dissapered. Nowadays, it keeps atraccting a lot of people from the surroundings of the town and the province.

It is traditional because its customs have been preserved in the town, transmitted from generation to generation.

One of the most important figures in "Mananta"(word which refers to the Puente Genil's Holy Week) is the romans

They were introduced years later the Mananta was invented and you can see them in the picture. They walk around the town and they represent the people who killed Jesus and carried him to the cross. There are like 250 romans which are really famous. It is something that all people want to see when they visit the town
